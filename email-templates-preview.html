CSBank Email Templates

Preview of all notification email templates used in the system

1. Email Verification

Trigger: Sent when a new user signs up

Purpose: Verify user's email address to activate account

2. New Due Created

Trigger: When a treasurer creates a new due

Recipients: All members of the group

3. Payment Verified

Trigger: When a treasurer verifies a payment

Recipients: The student who made the payment

4. Payment Rejected

Trigger: When a treasurer rejects a payment

Recipients: The student whose payment was rejected

5. Due Date Reminder

Trigger: Automatically sent X days before due date

Recipients: Students with unpaid dues

6. Password Reset

Trigger: When user requests password reset

Security: Link expires in 1 hour
